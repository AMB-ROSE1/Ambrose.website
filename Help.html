Welcome to the Hospital Data Portal Help Center!

  1. How to Log In
    • Use your Registered Username/ id and password to login
    • If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions
  2. Accessing your data
    • Once logged in, to the datshboard to view your relevant information
    • For doctors, access your patient's records,schedules,and test results from the "patient's records" section
    • For patients, you can view your medical history, appointments,and prescriptions
  3. Updating information
    • Doctors can update patient's records duriing consultations, including new prescriptions and diagnoes
    • Patients can request updates to their personal data under the "profile" section
  4. Security Tips:
    • Always log out after using the portal.
    • Do not share your password with anyone
    • Ensure your device has up-to-date security software
